Founded in April 2015, Okusinza E Manchester was born from a desire to unite Luganda-speaking followers of Jesus Christ in Manchester and across the United Kingdom.

What began as a small gathering of friends who wanted to worship God in their mother tongue has grown into a vibrant, loving community of over 160 members. We are Ugandan, British, and proudly Christian. We celebrate our faith and our culture together.

We meet every last Sunday of the month at Ascension Church Hulme, Manchester, and we welcome everyone — newcomers, visitors, families, young and old. Mujje mwenna — all are welcome.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a community where Luganda-speaking Christians in the UK can worship God freely in their mother tongue, grow in faith, support one another, and raise the next generation to know and love God.

We believe in the power of worship, the importance of family, and the strength of community. Okusinza mu luganda — worshipping from the heart, in our own language.

Welfare & Support

We care for every member of our community. If you or someone you know needs support in any way, please reach out. No one walks alone.
